# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Sales Performance Dashboard** built using **Power BI** to analyze sales, profit, customer performance, regional distribution, marketing effectiveness, and campaign results.

The dashboard helps businesses monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions by providing a comprehensive overview of sales and profitability.

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Features

### 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Total Profit:** 22.00K
- **Total Sales:** 107.77K
- **Profit Margin:** 0.20 (20%)

### 🌍 Regional Sales Analysis
Visualizes sales performance across different regions:
- South
- East
- North
- West

### 📅 Monthly Sales Trend
Tracks sales performance over time:
- January
- February
- March
- April
- May
- June

Provides insights into seasonal trends and growth patterns.

### 📢 Marketing Channel Performance
Analyzes sales contribution from various marketing channels:
- Referral
- Social Media
- Email
- SEO
- Ads

### 🎯 Campaign Profit Analysis
Compares profit generated by different marketing campaigns:
- Campaign A
- Campaign B
- Campaign C

### 👥 Customer Performance Table
Displays:
- Customer Name
- Region
- Sales
- Profit

Conditional formatting is used to highlight profitable and low-performing customers.

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### Sales Performance
- Total sales reached **107.77K**.
- Sales peaked during **March and June**.

### Profitability
- Overall profit generated was **22.00K**.
- Profit margin stands at **20%**.

### Regional Performance
- South region generated the highest sales.
- North and West regions showed comparatively lower sales.

### Marketing Effectiveness
- Referral marketing contributed the largest share of sales.
- SEO and Ads also played significant roles in revenue generation.

### Campaign Analysis
- Campaign B delivered the highest profit.
- Campaign C generated the lowest profit among the campaigns.
